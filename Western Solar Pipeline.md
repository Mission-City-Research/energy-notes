## Status Definitions

- **Announced** – Publicly disclosed, early development  
- **Permitting** – Actively pursuing permits / land approvals  
- **In Queue** – Interconnection request submitted  
- **Under Construction** – Construction started  
- **On Hold / Cancelled** – Development paused or withdrawn  

---

## Confidence Guide

- **High** – Construction or executed PPA  
- **Medium** – Permitting or advanced interconnection  
- **Low** – Announcement or early-stage queue position
